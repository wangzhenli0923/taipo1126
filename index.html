Empowering Recovery: Official Channels for the November 26, 2025 Tragedy

On November 26, 2025, a devastating fire broke out at Hung Fuk Estate, Tai Po, claiming many precious lives and leaving hundreds of families homeless. We extend our deepest condolences to all victims and their families.
Important Disclaimer: This website does NOT accept donations. We only collect and display officially verified donation channels with sources cited. Information may not be the very latest. Please always verify through official websites or hotlines before donating.
